Not your regular stopwatch

Runmill is more than a stopwatch. It is a fitness companion that helps you improve your physique in a smarter, connected way.

Extremely flexible

You can easily customize intervals on the app, so it doesn’t matter whether you’re a beginner or a professional athlete. Everyone’s welcome.

Check your heart rate in real-time

Runmill is completely integrated to the Apple Watch’s heart rate sensor so that you can quickly check your heart rate while exercising, which is critical to doing it safely, effectively and enjoyably.

Pro tip: for better results you want to workout at 75% of your maximum heart rate or above. To calculate 75% of your maximum heart rate:

220-your age = HRmax
HRmax x .75 = 75% of your HRmax

Improve your performance

Runmill will gently tap your wrist when it’s time to alternate from Workout to Recovery and vice-versa. Thanks to Apple’s Taptic Engine, your body is the user experience.
